Interesting facts about me.

Angela Hall

Developer/Artist

I am a systems and software engineer with close to 9 years of knowledge in various industries. Knowledgeable about both operations and engineering practices with the ability to implement best processes for users.

Driven in pride of ownership I have a thirst for knowlege of all aspects of engineering. Over the last few years I've been focused on gaining additional knowlege in Front End Development, Back End Development, and Devops practices. If it can be automated, I will certainly try to automate it. If it makes anyones life even a bit easier, it's definitely worth it. I aspire to become a full stack engineer or any title that will let me utilize all the skills I've learned in engineering.

Since a young age I've always been drawn to both art and computers. I taught myself how to use a majority of applications such as Dreamweaver, Photoshop, Illistrator, Visual Studio, and Eclipse to learn coding and design.

If I'm not working I'm usually painting, drawing, playing video games, or watching Netflix documentaries. I live in Chandler Arizona. I really enjoy camping during the times when Arizona weather is bearable. I love animals! I have 2 cats (Brie and Pepper) and 1 dog (LadyBird) who I love dearly.

Random fact: I'm really into Myers Briggs personality types. I am a proud INFJ. I can talk about it for hours and welcome anyone to a conversation.
